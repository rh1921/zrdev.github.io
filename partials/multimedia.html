Logos

Teams may use these logos for t-shirts, publicity, and other materials as long as:

  1. The Logos may not be modified in any way other than rendering in a monochromatic way; the images may not be trimmed, cropped, changed in aspect ratio, or modified in any other fashion.
  2. The only exception to (1) is that the logos of the sponsoring organizations (NASA, MIT, SSL, CASIS, Top Coder, MAP, Aurora, Northrop Grumman Foundation, and ESA) may be separated and placed elsewhere in the design. However these logos must appear in the design and cannot be re-ordered, re-shaped, trimmed, cropped, or modified in any way other than for monochromatic rendering.
  3. The Logos may not be used in any way that can be interpreted as the Zero Robotics program supporting any type of organization, company, or event other than the Zero Robotics competition the team is participating.
  4. The Logos may not be used in any way that can be interpreted as the Zero Robotics program giving preference to any team, alliance, school, or organization participating in the program.
